The Decentralized Credit Protocol

How does Ormi work

Borrower


  • Can take out variable-rate and stable-rate loans from existing liquidity pool with < 100% collateralization ratio.
  • Begin with overcollateralization (e.g. 110%), as the borrower builds up reputation with Ormi, borrowers' allowed collateralization ratio will be subsequently lowered (e.g.70%).
  • Option to provide past on-chain history at onboarding time to obtain upfront lower initial collateralization rate.
  • Default or liquidation results in unfavorable reputation, exponentially increasing required collateralization ratio.

Protocol


  • Debt restructuring mechanism via minting and selling Ormi tokens to cover deficit due to borrower default.
  • Smart treasury management, inflation without devaluation. Protocol ensures constant buy-back of Ormi token and to discretionarily inflate Ormi's supply without impacting Ormi token's value.
  • Protocol Owned Value/Liqudity, Ormi tokens are backed by a basket of assets via bonding and staking mechanisms.
  • Protocol based reputation/credit system built on Decentralized Identifiers/Verifiable Credentials.

Liquidity Provider/Lender


  • Provides liquidity to lending pools to earn yield.
  • Lending risk due to borrower default is primarily covered by protocol's debt restructuring mechanism.

Ormi DAO


  • Ormi token holders set the monetary policies, base interest rates, collateral assets, default risk parameter for the protocol.
  • Token holders policies directly affect solvency and default rate for the lending, hence directly affect Ormi token's value.
